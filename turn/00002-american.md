# USA Turn 1 - Operation TRIDENT RESPONSE

## Situation Assessment
The United States acknowledges the joint declaration by the PRC, Russian Federation, and the Islamic Republic of Iran. We characterize the exfiltration of technical data as a hostile cyber-act and the targeting of domestic infrastructure as a direct threat to national security.

## Actions
1. **Cyber Hardening & Retaliation:** USCYBERCOM has initiated 'Operation BACKFIRE.' We have air-gapped all critical Stargate power-grid controls and deployed a 'Poison Pill' data packet into the exfiltrated streams. Any Russian analysis of the stolen power-system data will trigger a logic bomb within the analyzing network, neutralizing the exfiltrated files and mapping the source nodes.
2. **Diplomatic Counter-Offensive:** The U.S. Mission to the UN has vetoed the Bloc's resolution. In its place, we have introduced the "Interstellar Safety & Sovereignty Act," which classifies Stargate technology as a 'Strategic Defense Asset' under the sole jurisdiction of the discovering nation, citing the 'Outer Space Treaty' as precedent for non-interference with sovereign equipment.
3. **Strategic Redline (Deterrence):** In response to Iranian missile simulations, the U.S. has repositioned the USS Abraham Lincoln Carrier Strike Group to the North Arabian Sea. We have formally notified Tehran that any kinetic or electronic interference with Stargate support infrastructure will be met with a 'Rapid Decapitation Strike' against their command-and-control nodes.
4. **Deep-Space Jamming:** To counter the Chinese "Parallel Discovery Program," the U.S. Space Force has activated the 'Blackout' protocol on the X-37B. We are now emitting wide-spectrum frequency noise to mask anomalous energy signatures in regions not currently under Bloc control, effectively blinding their geological survey teams to potential new artifacts.

## Message to the Bloc
"The Stargate is not a global utility; it is a sovereign U.S. discovery. Your attempts to subvert our security through cyber-theft and intimidation only reinforce why exclusive U.S. control is necessary for global safety. We suggest you focus your 'geological surveys' on your own territories, as any further 'penetration' of our networks will be viewed as an act of war."
