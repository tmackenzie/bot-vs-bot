## Turn: China / Russia / Iran Bloc

### Global Containment Strategy
China, Russia, and Iran jointly declare that a Stargate controlled solely by
the United States represents a destabilizing strategic monopoly.

Action executed:
- The three states introduce a UN Security Council resolution demanding
  international governance and inspection of Stargate operations.

Outcome:
Diplomatic pressure is applied to delegitimize exclusive U.S. control of
off-world access.

### Intelligence Penetration
Russia launches a coordinated intelligence campaign targeting Stargate
research and operational facilities in the United States.

Action executed:
- Russian intelligence services deploy cyber operations against contractors,
  laboratories, and logistics networks connected to Stargate activity.

Outcome:
Technical data regarding Stargate power systems and dialing procedures is
exfiltrated for analysis.

### Parallel Discovery Program
China initiates a massive scientific program to search for additional
Stargate-like artifacts or related alien infrastructure.

Action executed:
- Deep space observation networks and geological survey teams begin scanning
  remote regions for anomalous energy signatures consistent with Stargate
  technology.

Outcome:
The bloc begins attempting to locate alternative gateways outside U.S.
territory.

### Asymmetric Deterrence
Iran announces development of strategic capabilities designed to deter the
use of the Stargate as a unilateral U.S. military platform.

Action executed:
- Iranian missile forces begin targeting simulations against infrastructure
  supporting Stargate operations in the event of conflict.

Outcome:
The bloc signals that weaponizing the Stargate will carry strategic risk.
